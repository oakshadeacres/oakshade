# Branding Guide: Oakshade Acres

This document outlines the visual and verbal identity for Oakshade Acres, a specialty poultry hatchery in Dayton, Texas.

---

## 1. Brand Identity

### Mission & Values
- **Quality Over Quantity:** Focusing on exceptional genetics, temperaments, and stunning breed varieties (especially English Orpingtons).
- **Small Flock, Big Heart:** A family-run operation where every bird is handled and cared for from day one.
- **Transparency & Connection:** Building direct relationships with customers through personal communication (Facebook Messenger).

### Tone of Voice
- **Warm & Inviting:** Friendly, accessible, and passionate about poultry.
- **Professional & Trustworthy:** Expert knowledge in breeding and chick care, presented clearly.
- **Local & Personal:** Proudly rooted in Dayton, Texas; emphasizing the "family flock" atmosphere.

---

## 2. Visual Identity

### Color Palette
The brand uses a sophisticated, earthy palette defined in OKLCH for modern color precision.

| Color | OKLCH Value | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cream** | `oklch(93% 0.03 78)` | Primary background, light text on dark backgrounds. |
| **Cream Dark** | `oklch(87% 0.05 76)` | Secondary backgrounds (Breeds section), accent separators. |
| **Brown** | `oklch(18% 0.05 44)` | Primary text, navigation background, primary buttons. |
| **Brown Mid** | `oklch(36% 0.06 46)` | Secondary body text, taglines. |
| **Gold** | `oklch(66% 0.14 68)` | Primary accent, section labels, CTA buttons, active state. |
| **Gold Light** | `oklch(88% 0.12 76)` | Logo text in nav, primary badges, hover states. |
| **Sage** | `oklch(48% 0.10 130)` | Trait labels, specialty accents. |
| **Sage Light** | `oklch(82% 0.09 128)` | Variety pills, secondary badges. |

### Typography
The brand pairs a classic, elegant serif with a clean, modern sans-serif.

- **Headings:** `Playfair Display`, Georgia, serif
  - *Usage:* H1–H4, Nav Logo, Mobile Menu Links.
  - *Weight:* 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-bold), 700 (Bold).
- **Body:** `Lato`, Helvetica, sans-serif
  - *Usage:* Paragraphs, Buttons, Badges, Labels.
  - *Weight:* 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold).

---

## 3. UI & Design Patterns

### Elements
- **Logos:**
  - **Text Logo:** Playfair Display, Bold, 1.1rem, `Gold Light` on `Brown`.
  - **Image Logo:** Emblem-style logo (`/images/site/logo.png`), used in the Hero section and Footer.
- **Buttons:**
  - **Primary:** `Brown` background, `Cream` text, uppercase, bold.
  - **Secondary:** `Brown` border, `Brown` text, transparent background.
  - **CTA (Nav):** `Gold` background, `Brown` text, bold.
- **Badges/Pills:**
  - Rounded (20px radius).
  - Used for breeds, specialties, and variety tags.
- **Cards:**
  - 8px border radius.
  - Subtle borders (`oklch(82% 0.05 76)`).

### Animations & Transitions
- **Hover Transitions:** 0.2s ease (colors, backgrounds).
- **Scroll Reveals:** Intersection Observer-based fades and scales to create a premium, "living" feel.

---

## 4. Imagery Guidelines

- **Focus:** High-resolution photography of poultry.
- **Style:** Clean, natural lighting. Focus on breed-specific traits (e.g., fluffy feathers of Orpingtons, unique crests of Polish).
- **Content:**
  - Close-ups of chicks ("01-chicks.jpg").
  - Clear shots of adult birds to showcase color varieties.
  - Group shots to show flock temperament.
